About

JASMINE’S Restaurant was opened by award winning Chef Jasmine Parker to offer residents and visitors of souther Brooklyn with the authentic vegan cuisine that the Jasmine’s name has become synonymous with after the success of the Jasmine’s Restaurant in Las Vegas. The restaurant now has a loyal following of its own, offering traditional recipes created with health and taste in mind.
